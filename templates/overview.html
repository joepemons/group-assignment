Welcome to Fonteyn Holiday Park

Fonteyn Holiday Parks, established as a traditional Dutch family business by Frits Fonteyn, has evolved over 50 years into an international organization. Specializing in holiday park management, the company offers accommodation, recreational facilities, and various services across its locations in the Netherlands, Belgium, and Germany.
